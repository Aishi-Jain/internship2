Two long weeks I wandered

April 2, 2019 · Lifestyle

It had been two weeks since I left the city behind. The bustling noise, the constant race against time—it felt like a distant memory. In the solitude of these trails, I discovered a sense of peace I hadn't felt in years.

The days were long, each marked by a new discovery—a hidden stream, a meadow bursting with wildflowers, or a rocky peak offering panoramic views of the valley below.

But it wasn’t always easy. There were moments of doubt, when the path seemed uncertain and my spirit weary. Yet, the journey called to me, urging me forward.

"The journey isn't about the destination; it's about finding yourself along the way."

When I finally returned, I brought back more than memories. I carried a renewed sense of purpose and a deep appreciation for the simplicity and beauty of the natural world.
